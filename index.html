De Stefani Vincenzo’s Contribution to Italian Art Heritage

Homepage

This project explores Stefani Vincenzo’s impact on Italian Art, focusing on his paintings in Verona such as "Venezia Incipriata, Veduta di Città" and "Vespro, Scena di Vita Quotidiana."

Step 1

Our goal is to create SPARQL queries and compare two Large Language Models (LM) to enhance our research capabilities.

Step 2

Our goal is to add new data connections to the ArCo knowledge graph, making it more complete and informative about Vincenzo’s influence on Italian art.

Step 3

Our goal is to create a website on GitHub to showcase the findings and provide access to the enriched knowledge graph.

Report

Here, you will find the final report summarizing the project's findings, the effectiveness of the SPARQL queries, and insights gained from comparing the two LLMs.
